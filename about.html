Ruma Candles

Hello! Welcome to Ruma Candles. In 2022 I bought a candle making kit simply for something new to try one weekend and instantly fell in love with the process. I found it therapeutic, fun and enjoyed the specificity that making candles requires. I researched more into candle making with the aim to make some candles from scratch, outside of using a pre-made kit with the wax to scent ratios set out on a piece of paper. I bought some different waxes to experiment with, a variety of fragrance oils, containers and wicks and through trial and error (a lot of trial and error!) I began to learn the craft of handmade candles. I gave away some of my candles to family and friends I had lots of positive feedback. It was then that I decided to create a small candle business to run in my spare time.

There are some core values that were important to ensure that were integrated into the business from the start, I wanted to make the candles cruelty free, to be as environmentally friendly as possible and for the candles themselves to be handmade. With this in mind I chose a vegan soy wax for the candles, glass containers and cardboard packaging so that everything is fully recyclable. All of the candles are made by hand in my kitchen at home.

My background working in advertising has meant that I have been able to shoot and edit all of the product photography myself, as well as create the website. Ruma Candles was launched in 2023 selling on Etsy and at local markets. Thank you for shopping small.
